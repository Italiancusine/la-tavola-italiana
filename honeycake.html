Easy Honey Cake

A simple, sweet honey cake that's perfect for breakfast, dessert, or an afternoon tea. Soft, fragrant, and easy to make!

Ingredients (8 servings)

  • 1 cup (250 ml) honey
  • ½ cup (120 ml) vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups (190 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • Pinch of salt
  • ¼ cup (60 ml) milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8-inch round pan or line with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk honey, oil (or butter), eggs, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Gradually fold dry ingredients into the wet mixture. Add milk and stir until smooth.
  5. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake 35–40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Let cool before slicing. Optional: dust with powdered sugar or drizzle with extra honey.

Tips & Tricks

  • For a moist, denser cake, use all honey and a bit more oil.
  • Add nuts or dried fruits for variation.
  • Pairs beautifully with tea or coffee.
