What Makes Italian Olive Oil Great?

True extra virgin olive oil (EVOO) from Italy is cold-pressed, rich in antioxidants, and offers complex aromas like grass, artichoke, or tomato leaf. Its freshness, color, and clarity reflect traditional craftsmanship.

People Also Ask

What is the difference between virgin and extra virgin olive oil?

Extra virgin is cold-pressed and has the lowest acidity and best flavor. Virgin is slightly lower quality.

How do you know if olive oil is real?

Check for harvest date, dark bottle, and certification (like DOP or PGI).

Bitterness comes from polyphenols ‚Äî a sign of high-quality, fresh oil.

Should olive oil be refrigerated?

No. Keep it cool, but not cold. Refrigeration can cause cloudiness.

Is Italian olive oil better?

Italy is famous for diverse, artisanal oils. But good olive oil comes from Spain, Greece, and beyond too.
