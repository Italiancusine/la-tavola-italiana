Introduction

This Strawberry Basil Smoothie blends sweet berries and fragrant basil into a refreshing drink that's perfect for summer mornings or a midday boost. It’s creamy, cool, and uniquely flavorful.

History

While fruit smoothies became popular in the U.S. in the late 20th century, combining fruit with herbs like basil is a modern twist that originated in wellness-focused kitchens. Basil, traditionally used in savory Italian dishes, adds depth and herbal brightness to the sweetness of strawberries in this innovative fusion.

Why We Love It

This smoothie is a wonderful blend of sweet and herbal flavors, offering a refreshing and healthy way to start your day. Its creamy texture and natural sweetness make it a favorite for all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1/2 banana (for creaminess)
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or milk of choice)
  • 4–5 fresh basil leaves
  • 1–2 teaspoons honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness as needed.
  4. Pour into a glass and garnish with a basil leaf or strawberry slice if desired.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use frozen strawberries for a frostier smoothie without needing ice.
  • Substitute basil with mint for a fresh alternative.
  • Add chia seeds or protein powder for a nutritional boost.
  • If you don’t have yogurt, use a frozen banana instead for creaminess.

Serving Suggestions

Serve this smoothie as a breakfast drink with a slice of toast or alongside a light brunch. It also makes a great post-workout recovery drink thanks to its protein and antioxidants.

Nutrition Info (per serving)

  • Calories: 180
  • Protein: 7g
  • Carbohydrates: 28g
  • Fat: 3g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Sodium: 40mg

Fun Fact

Strawberries and basil might seem like an odd couple, but they're botanically related! Both belong to the Rosaceae (rose) family, and their flavors complement each other beautifully.
