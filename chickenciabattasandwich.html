Introduction

This Chicken Ciabatta Sandwich is a warm and hearty Italian-inspired meal, featuring grilled chicken breast, fresh arugula, sun-dried tomato spread, and melted mozzarella — all nestled inside a toasted ciabatta roll.

History

Ciabatta, meaning "slipper" in Italian due to its shape, was first developed in the 1980s in Veneto. Its airy interior and crisp crust make it ideal for panini and gourmet sandwiches like this flavorful grilled chicken version.

Why We Love It

It’s simple, filling, and deeply flavorful. The contrast of creamy mozzarella with tangy sun-dried tomato spread and juicy chicken makes this a go-to lunch or dinner. Plus, it’s easy to prep ahead!

Ingredients

  • 2 ciabatta rolls, halved
  • 2 grilled chicken breasts (or 1 large breast split in half)
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomato spread or pesto
  • 4 slices fresh mozzarella
  • 1 cup arugula
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat a skillet or panini press over medium heat.
  2. Slice ciabatta rolls and brush inside with olive oil.
  3. Grill or reheat chicken breasts; season with salt and pepper if needed.
  4. Spread sun-dried tomato spread on the bottom halves of the ciabatta rolls.
  5. Layer with chicken, mozzarella, and arugula.
  6. Top with the other half of the roll and press slightly.
  7. Toast in the skillet or press until cheese is melted and bread is crisp.
  8. Slice and serve warm.

Tips & Tricks

  • Marinate chicken beforehand in olive oil, lemon, and herbs for more flavor.
  • Add roasted red peppers or caramelized onions for variation.
  • Use a grill pan with a weight to mimic a panini press.

Serving Suggestions

Pair with a side salad of mixed greens and a glass of sparkling water or iced tea. Perfect for a casual Italian-style lunch.

Fun Fact

Ciabatta bread was Italy’s direct response to the popularity of French baguettes, aiming to create a uniquely Italian sandwich bread.
