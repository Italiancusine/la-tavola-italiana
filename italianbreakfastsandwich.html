Introduction

This Italian Breakfast Sandwich layers creamy scrambled eggs, fresh arugula, melted provolone, and juicy tomato slices on a crusty ciabatta roll. A perfect way to start your morning, the sandwich offers rustic comfort with Italian flair.

History

Though breakfast sandwiches originated in the U.S., Italians have long embraced rustic breads with savory fillings. This modern creation takes inspiration from Italian café culture where a small morning panino and espresso are a staple.

Why We Love It

It’s quick, nourishing, and full of flavor. The mix of soft eggs, peppery greens, melty cheese, and crispy bread feels indulgent yet wholesome—just like a morning in Rome.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 ciabatta roll, halved and toasted
  • 2 slices provolone cheese
  • 1/4 cup arugula
  • 2 tomato slices

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in eggs and stir gently until softly scrambled.
  3. Place cheese slices on the warm eggs to melt slightly.
  4. Layer the bottom half of the toasted roll with arugula and tomato.
  5. Top with the cheesy scrambled eggs and the top half of the roll.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use a nonstick pan for best scrambled egg texture.
  • Ciabatta rolls can be toasted with a bit of garlic butter for extra flavor.
  • Substitute provolone with mozzarella or fontina for variation.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with a cappuccino or a fresh orange juice. Add olives or a small side salad for a fuller brunch.

Fun Fact
